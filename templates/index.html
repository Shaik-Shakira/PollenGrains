About Us

Our platform is dedicated to advancing the field of palynology through innovative technology. We provide tools for researchers, botanists, and students to accurately classify various pollen grains, aiding in studies related to allergies, environmental monitoring, forensic science, and paleobotany.

Leveraging cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, we aim to simplify the complex process of pollen identification, making scientific research more accessible and efficient. Our goal is to contribute to a deeper understanding of plant life and its impact on our world.
